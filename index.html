I'm Nico — an application security engineer based in Mendoza, Argentina.
Lucky enough to live at the foot of the Andes — my weekends
usually involve them.

I started as a Java backend developer. After a few years building APIs
and integrations — including a stint in fintech — I crossed to the
other side of the terminal and moved into cybersecurity. Turns out
understanding how systems are built is a pretty good foundation for
figuring out how they break.

Today I work in application security — DevSecOps, security automation,
SAST/DAST, and lots of code reading. I care about making security
something engineering teams actually want to engage with, not something
that slows them down.

When I'm not in front of a screen, I'm probably on a mountain.
I race trail running, love travelling to new places, and
play football. I also like music a lot, sometimes I try to
play bass and I really enjoy learning stuff I feel connected to.
